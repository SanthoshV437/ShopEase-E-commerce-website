Getting Started with ShopEase

Your complete guide to setting up and launching your online store.

1. Create Your Account

Sign up on our platform using your email. Verify your account to activate your dashboard.

2. Set Up Your Store

Add your store name, choose a template, and customize your brand look with colors and images.

3. Add Products

Upload product images, set prices, write strong descriptions, and organize items into categories.

4. Configure Payments

Connect secure payment methods like UPI, credit/debit cards, and wallets for fast checkout.

5. Launch Your Store

Preview your website and go live! Start sharing your store link with customers instantly.
